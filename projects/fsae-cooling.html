FSAE Cooling Sleeves

Custom cooling jackets for FSAE electric in-hub motors, optimized with parametric CFD in Ansys Fluent and designed for metal 3D printing.

Ansys FluentCFDMetal 3D Printing

Overview

To improve thermal performance, I engineered custom cooling jackets for our FSAE electric in-hub motors. Utilizing my experience in CFD and Fluid Dynamics, I conducted parametric simulations in Ansys Fluent to optimize heat dissipation and designed the final parts for metal 3D printing, ensuring both peak performance and manufacturability.
